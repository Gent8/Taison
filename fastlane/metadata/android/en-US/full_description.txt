Taison is a manga reader, refined to deliver a clean, highly organized experience without extra weight.

• Flexible navigation – Choose tabs or dropdowns to move through your library.
• Organized history – History is scoped and navigable the same way as your library, keeping active groups in sync.
• Taison look – A distinct, warm palette.

More Taison features:

• Group-aware filters for categories, sources, status, and ungrouped items.
• Customizable category visibility and editable default category names.
• History eye toggle to show or hide items outside your library, with scoped or full deletions.
